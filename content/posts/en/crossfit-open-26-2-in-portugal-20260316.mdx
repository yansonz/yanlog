I've been staying in Atouguia da Baleia for about 20 days and completed 26.2 and 26.3 at [CrossFit Lusíadas](https://maps.app.goo.gl/je51tn3nDu3fpMCS9).

Watching the Open workout announcement live is usually impossible in Korea since it's early morning, but being in Europe meant I could watch it during evening hours. Watching David Castro get all excited by himself while announcing each workout made him look even more annoying.

![Watching 26.2 announcement live](https://images.yanbert.com/yanlog/images/crossfit-open-26-2-in-portugal-20260316/IMG_7575.webp)

He revealed the 26.2 movements one by one—pull-ups, chest-to-bar, muscle-ups—building anticipation. Then he said he hadn't specified which type of muscle-up and showed two rings overlapping. Turns out ring muscle-ups appeared in early Open workouts like 12.4, 14.4, and 15.3, but recently it's been mostly bar muscle-ups, so nobody expected this.

> **26.2 WORKOUT**
>
> For time:  
> &nbsp;80-foot dumbbell overhead walking lunge  
> &nbsp;20 alternating dumbbell snatches  
> &nbsp;20 pull-ups  
> &nbsp;80-foot dumbbell overhead walking lunge  
> &nbsp;20 alternating dumbbell snatches  
> &nbsp;20 chest-to-bar pull-ups  
> &nbsp;80-foot dumbbell overhead walking lunge  
> &nbsp;20 alternating dumbbell snatches  
> &nbsp;20 muscle-ups  
> Time cap: 15 minutes  
>
> ♂ 50-lb (22.5-kg) dumbbell

I practiced ring muscle-ups last year. High reps are tough, but I can get one when exhausted. So I figured if I paced myself to reach the muscle-ups, I'd have a shot. My strategy from the start: break everything up even if I could go unbroken (switch hands every 8m on lunges, pull-ups in sets of 10, C2B in sets of 5).

> **Score: 113 reps [Rx'd]**
>
> Details:  
> &nbsp;2 rounds +  
> &nbsp;80-ft DB lunge  
> &nbsp;20 DB snatches  
> &nbsp;1 ring muscle-up  
> &nbsp;Tiebreak: 12:34

The pacing strategy worked. I started struggling on the second set of dumbbell snatches. C2B didn't go as planned—broke them into singles. The last set of snatches took three breaks to finish 20 reps. Still, I hit the muscle-ups at 12:34, leaving time to catch my breath and attempt them multiple times. Got 1 out of 3 attempts! Last year's practice paid off. Super satisfied with this result.

![Ring muscle-up success](https://images.yanbert.com/yanlog/images/crossfit-open-26-2-in-portugal-20260316/ring-muscle-up-success.webp)

![CrossFit Open 26.2 Masters Men 40-44 Division Analysis](https://images.yanbert.com/yanlog/images/crossfit-open-26-2-in-portugal-20260316/26-2-PCT-Masters-Men.webp)

Looking at the 40-44 division, the median is 112 reps (failing the muscle-up). That one muscle-up makes a huge difference in rankings. Makes up for my weak 26.1 performance.
