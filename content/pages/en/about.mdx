# Hello, I'm YAN SO.

I work in the AI/ML and data-related fields.

## Key Milestones
- Career activities are updated on [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/yanso/).
  - **Head of AI/Data** | Kakao Style | 2017~2026 | Led the AI and data organization at Kakao Style, which operates [Zigzag](https://zigzag.kr), a women's fashion e-commerce service.
  - **Data Scientist** | Urbanbase | 2016~2017 | Developed image object recognition models and 3D rendering software for a solution that converts 2D real estate floor plans into 3D.
  - **MSc. Data Science** | University of Auckland | 2015~2016 | Completed a master's degree in data science at the University of Auckland in New Zealand and returned to Korea.
  - **Research Engineer** | LG Electronics | 2010~2015 | Responsible for middleware software development for European Digital TVs and broadcast certification in European countries.
- Since 2018, I have been participating and serving as an organizer in the [AWS Korea User Group](https://www.awskr.org).

- Since 2020, I have been selected as an [AWS Machine Learning Hero](https://aws.amazon.com/blogs/aws/get-to-know-the-latest-aws-heroes-including-the-first-iot-heroes/).
  - The title changed to AI Hero starting in 2025.
